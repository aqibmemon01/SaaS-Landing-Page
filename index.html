Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any technical skills to use Yuplify?

No technical skills required! Yuplify was built specifically for non-technical users. Our drag-and-drop editor makes it easy to create a professional eCommerce store without writing a single line of code.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes! You can connect your existing domain name to your Yuplify store or purchase a new domain directly through our platform. We handle all the technical aspects of domain setup for you.

What payment methods can I offer to my customers?

Yuplify integrates with all major payment processors including PayPal, Stripe, Square, and many more. You can offer credit card payments, digital wallets, and even alternative payment methods depending on your location.

Can I migrate my existing online store to Yuplify?

Absolutely! We offer migration services to help you transfer your products, customers, and order history from platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and others. Our team will assist you throughout the process.

Is there a limit to how many products I can sell?

The number of products you can sell depends on your plan. Our Starter plan supports up to 100 products, Professional supports up to 500, and Enterprise offers unlimited products.

What kind of support does Yuplify offer?

All plans include 24/7 customer support through email and chat. Professional and Enterprise plans also include priority support and dedicated account managers to help you grow your business.
